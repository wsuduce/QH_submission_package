k(z) evolution for different time coordinates:

e-fold time (u = ln a):
k ≈ 0.530 ± 0.005 across z ∈ [4.5, 7.5]
Curvature metric: 0.002 (minimal trend)

Conformal time (η):
k: 0.564 → 0.474 (16% decrease)
Curvature metric: 0.080 (significant trend)

H₀-normalized lookback:
k: 0.535 → 0.445 (17% decrease)  
Curvature metric: 0.120 (strong trend)

Only e-fold preserves single-k property across redshift range.
Alternative time coordinates break parameter-free β/α→k mapping.
